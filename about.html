About

Sause is a streetwear clothing brand founded in Detroit, MI, designed to inspire style, confidence and humbleness. We don't want to create over confidence like high end brands and endorse our customers to get "Lost in the sause" and lose their humility. We strive to create a better world for everyone in the fashion industry and deliver top quality customer service by keeping it real, fun and sausy. Our mission is to sause up societal norms and become the world's favorite apparel one drip at a time.
